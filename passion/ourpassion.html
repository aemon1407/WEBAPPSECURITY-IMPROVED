OUR PASSION

OBJECTIVES

Enhancing the capabilities and self-reliance of students to enable them to lead communities and the nation.

Establishing and supporting initiatives that position universities as centers of knowledge contributing to the nation.

Building the identity of students based on a genuine and comprehensive understanding of Islam.

VISION

The builder fosters comprehensive Islamic youth leadership for the country through the involvement of student activities towards the year 2030.

MISSION

Nurturing students with a spirit of love for the country and a commitment to strengthen the national identity.
